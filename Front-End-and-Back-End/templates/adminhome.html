About OurSelf

From city streets to village roads, KSRTC connects lives through dependable travel.

Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) is a government-owned public transport organization in Kerala. It provides bus services connecting cities, towns, villages, and remote areas across the state and also operates interstate services. KSRTC plays an important role in daily travel by offering affordable and reliable transportation for students, workers, and tourists. It supports Kerala’s economy and tourism by ensuring safe and accessible public transport for everyone
